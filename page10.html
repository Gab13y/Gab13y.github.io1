How to Send Money through Fortune Pay

Transferring funds is made easy through Fortune Pay.
There are two ways to do this:

1. Via QR

a. Open your Fortune Pay app.
b. Tap on “Transfer”.
c. Have the recipient tap on “My QR”.
d. Using your phone’s camera, scan the QR code of the recipient’s Fortune Pay account.
e. Input amount and click next.
f. If successful, confirmation will be sent via SMS. 

2. Via Mobile number

a. Open your Fortune Pay app.
b. Tap on “Transfer”.
c. Enter mobile number of the recipient and click next.
d. Input the amount.
e. Check your notifications for confirmation.

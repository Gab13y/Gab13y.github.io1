How to Shop using
Fortune Pay

Shopping is made easy with Fortune Pay. Just shop with any of our merchant partners and follow these steps:

1. After selecting the items to be purchased, line up at the counter.

2. Open your Fortune Pay app.

3. Tap on “Scan QR”.

4. Scan the merchant’s QR code.

5. Input the amount to be paid.

6. Check your notifications for confirmation.
